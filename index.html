About Me

I am a passionate and skilled Full Stack Software Engineer with a diverse range of expertise. With a strong background in Odoo development, I excel in both front-end and back-end technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Django, Node.js, React, and the Odoo Framework.

I take pride in crafting elegant and user-friendly interfaces using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I am constantly expanding my knowledge to stay up to date with the latest front-end technologies. On the back-end side, I am well-versed in Python and Django, utilizing these technologies to build robust and secure server-side applications.

Prior to my software engineering career, I spent two years working as a Radio Network Planning and Optimization Engineer. During this time, I developed a strong expertise in planning and optimization tools such as Atoll, Genex Assistant, TEMS Investigation, and Actix Analyzer. I honed my skills in analyzing network performance, identifying optimization opportunities, and implementing strategies to enhance network quality and efficiency.

I am deeply passionate about technology and its potential to drive innovation. I am constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to expand my knowledge and skill set. With a strong foundation in software engineering and network optimization, I am confident in my ability to tackle complex projects and deliver outstanding results.
